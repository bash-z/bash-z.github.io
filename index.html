AI Poetry Generator

Used a LSTM-based deep network architecture with a mechanism for evaluating the phonetic content of a generated rhyme. Later enhanced model with GPT-2. View some of the AI generated poems here.

COVID-19 TF-IDF+BM25 Information Retrieval System

Created an NLP based search engine for papers related to COVID-19. Used TF-IDF and inverted indices to organize our data, and then used Okapi BM25 to rank the dataset’s research paper relevance to any particular search query. Submitted for a Kaggle competition.
